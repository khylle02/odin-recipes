Adobong Chicken

Chicken Adobo is a type of Filipino chicken stew. Chicken pieces are marinated in soy sauce and spices, pan-fried, and stewed until tender. The dish gained popularity because of its delicious taste and ease in preparation.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Garlic
  • Dark soy sauce
  • White vinegar

Steps

  1. First heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a big pot. I used a Dutch oven.
  2. Add the garlic and chicken thighs, skin side down.
  3. Once done searing the chicken, add the oyster sauce and soy sauce. Mix to the coat the chicken.
  4. Add the vinegar, brown sugar, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Bring the liquid to a boil and lower to a simmer.
