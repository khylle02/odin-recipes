Jollibee Fried Chicken

Jollibee-style Fried Chicken (Homemade Chicken Joy) is definitely one of, if not the most popular fried chicken here in the Philippines. It has that taste that captures the attention of Filipinos and its enticing aroma surely makes you think about the phrase, langhap sarap.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs, or any other chicken part
  • ¼ cup lemon juice, optional
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 cups cooking oil (for deep-frying), or as needed

Steps

  1. (if using lemon juice) Rub the chicken thighs with lemon juice and season with salt and pepper. Marinate for 30 to 60 minutes.
  2. n a mixing bowl, add-in the all-purpose flour, cornstarch, five-spice powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Mix until evenly incorporated.
  3. In a deep cooking pot, pour-in and heat cooking oil over high heat.
  4. Fully coat the chicken thighs with the flour mixture, and then add into the cooking pot. Cook for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown and crispy. Avoid overcrowding the pot.
  5. Transfer to a serving plate or serving tray and dry excess oil with paper towels. Serve and enjoy!
